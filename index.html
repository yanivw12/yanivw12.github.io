Yaniv Wolf

I am a graduate student at the Technion CS faculty, specializing in 3D computer vision.

My research focuses on 3D Gaussian Splatting, surface reconstruction, and novel view synthesis. I am passionate about developing efficient and accurate methods for 3D scene understanding and representation.

Publications

  1. GS2Mesh: Surface Reconstruction from Gaussian Splatting via Novel Stereo Views Yaniv Wolf*, Amit Bracha*, Ron Kimmel
    Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, Haifa, Israel     ECCV 2024 paper project page code
    TL;DR: We present a fast and accurate method to reconstruct smooth, geometrically-consistent surfaces from Gaussian Splatting models, by utilizing the superior novel view synthesis capabilities of GS and the accuracy of stereo correspondence models for depth estimation.
